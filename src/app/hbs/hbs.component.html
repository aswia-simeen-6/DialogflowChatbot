HUMANITIES AND BASIC SCIENCES

This department plays a vital role in guiding the students so that there is a smooth transition from the study they had at 10 + 2 level to the study they undergo in Engineering and Technology. Teachers of this department belong to different disciplines namely Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry. Department is having well qualified and experienced faculty and supporting staff. The faculty is actively involved in research work, publishing papers in various journals. The Department is recognized for carrying research work leading to award of Doctoral and M.Phil. Degrees. Department conducts seminars and workshops for the benefit of students and faculty on interdisciplinary and general topics of contemporary importance.

The Department has two well-equipped laboratories such as Chemistry and Physics and accommodates 60 students at a time and covers 28 experiments. The laboratories have all the necessary infrastructure not only to train the students as per the curriculum, but also to meet the requirements of Research and Consultancy activities.

Humanities

The Department of Humanities was established at the inception of the college in 1985. The department has qualified and experienced faculty and some of them have Post Graduate Diploma in the Teaching of English from English and Foreign Languages University (IFLU), Hyderabad.
