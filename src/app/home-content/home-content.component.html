Admission Procedure

Category A : 70% of seats will be filled by the Government of Andhra Pradesh as per the norms approved.

Category B : 15% of Non-NRI seats will be filled by the Government of Andhra Pradesh as per the norms approved. The remaining 15% of NRI seats will be filled by the College as per the norms approved by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Fee Details:

The Fee Structure for Category-A Admission Seats is Rs. 77,000. ( For All Branches)

The Fee Structure for Category-B Admission Seats is Rs. : 2,31,000 for CSE, CSB, CSM, CSD.

RS 2,00,000 for ECE and Rs. 1,00,000 for EEE, MEC, CIV.

Scholarship Details

The college offers Complete Tuition Fee Waiver for all the FOUR YEARS of the B.Tech course to students who join this college with less than 2000 rank in EAPCET.
